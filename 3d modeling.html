3D MODELING

As a 3D model artist, I specialize in creating high-quality 3D assets, including modeling, texturing, rigging, and animation. My work supports a wide range of applications such as video games, animated productions, 3D printing, product visualization, advertising, and more.

Services:

  • 3D Modeling: Creation of characters, environments, props, and products.
  • Texturing & UV Mapping: Applying realistic materials, PBR textures, and clean UV layouts.
  • Rigging: Building character, facial, and mechanical rigs for animation.
  • 3D Animation: Character motions, product animations, and mechanical animations.
  • Rendering & Visualization: High-quality, photorealistic renders for products, ads, and presentations.
  • 3D Sculpting: High-detail digital sculpts suitable for games, cinematics, and 3D printing.
  • Game-Ready Asset Creation: Low-poly modeling, texture baking, and engine-optimized assets.
  • VR/AR Asset Development: Optimized models for immersive and interactive applications.
  • Product Visualization: Realistic 3D product mockups for marketing, presentations, and advertising.
  • Model Editing & Modification: Updating, refining, optimizing, or correcting previously created 3D models.
